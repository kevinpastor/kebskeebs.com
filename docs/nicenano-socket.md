# 5. nice!nano Socket (Mill-Max)

![Mill-Max female sockets inserted into the PCB](../assets/05-millmax-sockets-placed.jpg)
![Mill-Max sockets soldered from the back](../assets/05-millmax-sockets-soldered.jpg)

The Mill-Max female sockets let you remove the nice!nano later without desoldering. Install two 12-pin female headers (315-43-112-41-003000) on the **top side** of the PCB.

1. Insert both 12-pin female headers into the nice!nano footprint on the top side of the PCB.
2. To keep them aligned while soldering, insert the 12-pin pin headers (350-10-112-00-006000) into the sockets, then rest the nice!nano on top. This holds everything straight.
3. Flip the PCB over.
4. Solder the two corner pins of each socket first to lock them in place.
5. Check that the sockets are flush and straight, then solder the remaining pins.
6. Remove the pin headers and nice!nano. Do not solder them yet.

> **Warning:** Keep your iron at 300–320 °C for this step. The plastic housing can deform if overheated.

> **Verify:** The sockets should be flush against the PCB and not tilted. Check all 24 joints for clean, shiny joints and no bridges.

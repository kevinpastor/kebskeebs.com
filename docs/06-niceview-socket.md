# 6. nice!view Socket

![nice!view socket inserted into the PCB](../assets/06-niceview-socket-placed.jpg)
![nice!view socket soldered](../assets/06-niceview-socket-soldered.jpg)

The nice!view socket (5-pin) goes on the **top side** of the PCB.

1. Insert the 5-pin female socket into the nice!view footprint on the top side of the PCB.
2. Optionally secure it with a piece of tape.
3. Flip the PCB over and solder one pin.
4. Check that the socket is straight and flush, then solder the remaining 4 pins.

# 10. nice!view

![nice!view pin headers soldered to the display](../assets/10-niceview-headers.jpg)
![nice!view inserted into the PCB socket](../assets/10-niceview-installed.jpg)

1. Insert the **short side** of the 5-pin header into the underside of the nice!view. The long side will plug into the socket on the PCB.
2. Solder one pin, check that the header is straight and flush with the nice!view, then solder the remaining 4 pins.
3. Plug the nice!view into the socket on the PCB.

> **Note:** The display cover has not been designed yet. Leave the nice!view inserted for now and revisit once the cover is available.
